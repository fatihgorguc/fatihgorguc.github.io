AGENT IN DEPTH
An instant-death fast-paced action platformer

  • Team Size: 7 (2 Developers, 2 Designers, 1 Artist, 1 Composer, 1 Sound Designer) + 2 External Collaborators
  • Platforms: Steam & Itch.io (Windows, Linux, macOS)
  • Developed by: Naturepixel Studios
  • Published by: Next in Game
  • Release Date: January 2023
  • Engine: Unity

About the Game

Agent in Depth is a high-octane action-platformer that tests the limits of your agility and combat skills. Run, jump, and wield your katana to deflect bullets and destroy enemies in a relentless, instant-death environment. With no checkpoints and no upgrades, every movement counts and every decision is crucial. Embrace the challenge as you deflect, die, learn, and repeat to become faster and more adept.
